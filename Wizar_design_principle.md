About the "Wizar" Identity

I also think your explanation is valuable from a cultural perspective.

Rather than claiming supernatural powers, the project can use "Wizar" as a symbolic educational archetype.

In Burmese tradition, figures such as Boe Boe Aung are associated with wisdom, symbols, moral teaching, and folklore. Different people hold different beliefs about these traditions, and many stories have been passed down through generations. Your project can respectfully draw inspiration from the cultural symbolism—curiosity, learning, and ethical responsibility—without requiring readers to accept any particular supernatural claim.

That gives your engine a distinctly Burmese identity while keeping it open and educational.
